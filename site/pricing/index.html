Questions

What's included in the free trial?
Full access to amux Cloud for 7 days. No credit card required to start. You'll be prompted to add a payment method before the trial ends.
Do I need my own API key?
Yes. amux Cloud provides the infrastructure, but you bring your own Anthropic API key. You can add it in settings after signing up.
Can I switch between monthly and annual?
Yes. You can switch plans anytime from your billing settings. Upgrades are prorated, downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
What happens when I cancel?
Your sessions and data remain accessible until the end of your billing period. After that, your container is paused. Data is retained for 30 days.
Is the self-hosted version feature-complete?
Yes. amux is fully open source. Cloud adds managed hosting, persistence, and sharing — the core features are identical.
