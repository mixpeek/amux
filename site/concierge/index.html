Dental practices

Start with insurance predetermination & eligibility

Verifying benefits, assembling predetermination packets, and chasing payers commonly eats 10 to 20+ hours a week per location. We take that paperwork off your team so they can focus on patients.

Live · predetermination workflow
What we automate first
  • Pull real-time eligibility and benefits (X12 270/271) before the patient sits down.
  • Assemble payer-specific predetermination packets: radiographs, perio charting, clinical notes, and procedure codes.
  • Draft the clinical narrative for your team to review.
  • Submit through your existing clearinghouse or payer portal, then poll for status so nobody babysits a fax.
  • Flag denials early and prep the resubmission or appeal.
  • Post the estimate back into the patient record and ledger.

Skilled nursing facilities

Start with referral intake & census

This is the work that happens on nights and weekends, and it decides your occupancy. PointClickCare runs about 60% of U.S. skilled nursing, and nurses can spend nearly 40% of a shift documenting. We take the keying, chasing, and reporting off their plate.

Live · referral intake workflow
What we automate first
  • Ingest referrals from fax, email, and hospital-discharge packets, structure them for review, and pull the insurance details.
  • Keep a live census and bed-board, and generate occupancy and payer-mix reports for owners and operators.
  • Track MDS / RAI assessment windows and pre-fill drafts from the existing chart for your MDS coordinator to verify.
  • Draft and summarize progress notes for clinician review.
  • Push the numbers that matter to a dashboard you check from your phone.
