Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

amux is an open-source, local-first application. Your privacy is fundamental to how amux is designed. This policy covers both the self-hosted open-source software and the amux.io website.

Self-hosted amux (open source)

When you run amux on your own machine:

  • No data leaves your machine. amux runs entirely locally. All session data, board items, notes, and CRM contacts are stored on your local filesystem.
  • No telemetry. amux does not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage data of any kind.
  • No accounts. There is no sign-up, no login, and no user tracking.
  • No third-party services. amux makes no outbound network requests except those you explicitly configure (e.g., S3 calendar sync).
  • Your code stays yours. amux manages Claude Code sessions via tmux. Your source code, prompts, and agent outputs never pass through amux servers because there are no amux servers.

amux.io website

The amux.io marketing website:

  • Is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages.
  • Does not use cookies or tracking scripts.
  • Does not collect personal information.
  • Uses Google Fonts, which may log IP addresses per Google's privacy policy.

amux iOS app

The amux iOS app is a thin native wrapper (WKWebView) that connects to your self-hosted amux server or cloud.amux.io:

  • No data collection. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to amux or any third party.
  • Server URL only. The only data stored locally on your device is the URL of your amux server, saved in UserDefaults.
  • No analytics. The app contains no analytics SDKs, ad frameworks, or tracking code.
  • Self-signed certificates. The app accepts self-signed TLS certificates for Tailscale and local network connections to your own server.

cloud.amux.io (managed cloud)

If you use the managed cloud service at cloud.amux.io:

  • Authentication is handled via your identity provider. We store your email address for account identification.
  • Session data, board items, and notes are stored on cloud infrastructure and associated with your account.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize your data.
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing support@amux.io.

Data retention

For self-hosted: you control all data. Delete it anytime from your filesystem (~/.amux/).

For cloud: data is retained while your account is active. Upon deletion request, all data is purged within 30 days.

Children's privacy

amux is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
